Bolstering Glasgow as Scotland’s Rocketry Hub

Glasgow’s next student-run rocket lab — designing, building, and launching the next generation of aerospace talent.

Our Mission

“Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of engineers by advancing hands-on expertise in rocketry and promoting awareness of the vast opportunities within the UK’s space industry.”

Objectives

  • Inspire students & public by highlighting aerospace opportunities.
  • Provide a multidisciplinary platform for practical engineering, business & communication skills.
  • Build pathways to internships through industry partnerships.
  • Compete in national competitions (Mach 25) fostering innovation.
